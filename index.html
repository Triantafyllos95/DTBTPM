Welcome!

This is a website that will introduce you to IT/IS Project Management, through some case studies, examples and games.

There is an Introduction, 2 Cases to study, quizzes at the end of every section, the conclusion and in the "useful links" link, you can play games related to the Topic.

This will be an interactive and fun way to learn about IT/IS Project Management which will help you understand what to do and what not to do when you managing an IT project.
